# Toastmasters International Brand Guidelines (Web Summary)

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Loyal Blue | #004165 | Headers, backgrounds, primary |
| True Maroon | #772432 | Accents, headers, CTAs |
| Cool Gray | #A9B2B1 | Backgrounds, secondary |
| Happy Yellow | #F2DF74 | Highlights, accent only |
| White | #FFFFFF | Background, text on dark |
| Black | #000000 | Text |

**Gradients (Loyal Blue):** `linear-gradient(135deg, #004165, #006094)`
**Gradients (True Maroon):** `linear-gradient(135deg, #3B0104, #781327)`

Never place the logo on colors outside the brand palette.

## Fonts (Web)

| Font | Role | Google Fonts |
|---|---|---|
| Montserrat | Headlines, subheads (free Gotham alternative) | `family=Montserrat:wght@400;600;700;800` |
| Source Sans 3 | Body copy (free Myriad Pro alternative) | `family=Source+Sans+3:wght@400;600` |

Google Fonts link tag:
```html
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Montserrat:wght@400;600;700;800&family=Source+Sans+3:wght@400;600&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
```

Do NOT use word art, drop shadows, or other font manipulations.

## Logo

- Use the official Toastmasters International logo -- do not create custom club logos
- Clubs are NOT permitted to create their own logo, theme, or tagline
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the wordmark on all sides
- Minimum web size: 72px wide
- Do not place logo on non-brand colors
- Do not overlap text or images on the logo
- Do not alter proportions, colors, or any element

**Official logo download:** https://www.toastmasters.org/resources/brand-portal
**Typical hosted URL pattern:** Upload via FTH media manager and use the resulting URL.

For HTML templates, use this placeholder: `{{LOGO_URL}}`
Fallback if no URL available: Link to the official TI website logo as a last resort, or
instruct user to upload the logo via FTH media manager and replace the URL.

## Approved Phrases (use one per page)

- Find Your Voice
- Relax, present confidently.
- Relax, speak confidently.
- Communicate Confidently(r)
- Find your confidence
- Become a better leader
- Invest in a Brighter Future

These phrases work well as hero taglines or section headings.

## Required Website Disclaimer

This text MUST appear at the bottom of every club website:

> "The information on this website is for the sole use of Toastmasters' members, for
> Toastmasters business only. It is not to be used for solicitation and distribution of
> non-Toastmasters material or information."

## Voice and Tone

All content should be: warm, friendly, clear, respectful, professional, succinct.
Avoid jargon. Write in second person ("you", "your").

## Photography / Images

- Show people in Toastmasters environments: meetings, speeches, networking
- Avoid: landscapes, animals, children, food, clip art as main elements
- Always use images you have permission to use
